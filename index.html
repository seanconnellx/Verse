We are a company that does good.
Our impact so far.
Radio Tagging Atlantic Salmon
Scottish Highlands, UK
In the Scottish Highlands, we worked with the local fisheries trust to tag and track salmon to identify critical barriers to their migration upriver and potential solutions.
Monitoring Wildlife at Alladale Wilderness Reserve
Scotland, UK
We worked with Alladale to carry out a camera trapping survey of the reserve, providing support with data processing and analysis. The data collected will help the team better understand, and therefore protect, the species that inhabit the reserve.
Saving Rare Sea Cliff Plants in Portugal
Portugal
On a stunning 15 km stretch of Portuguese coast two special cliff plants found nowhere else on earth are facing extinction. To address the challenges they are facing, we are organising invasive plant removal events.
Restoring Californias Sea Otter Populations
California, USA
We are funding a research project into the impacts of human disturbance on sea otters in California. Sea otters are a keystone species that are vital for the health of kelp forests, but are vulnerable to human disturbances. To better understand this threat and how to address it, we are funding a local university student to analyse camera trap data collected on marine recreation users.
